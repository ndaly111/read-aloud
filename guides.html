Before you paste text

  • Use text you have the rights to use. (Examples: your own writing, public domain text, permission-based material.)
  • Be mindful with sensitive content. If you’re on a shared device or screen‑sharing, avoid pasting private text.
  • Voice availability varies. Different browsers/OSes have different built‑in voices.
