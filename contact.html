Contact

Reach out for support, feedback, or partnership ideas. Read‑Aloud is a small project; clear details help us reply quickly.

Response time

We aim to respond within two business days. Complex reports that require reproducing device-specific issues may take a bit longer. If you are reporting a time-sensitive classroom issue, mention the date so we can triage accordingly.

Bug report template

Copy and paste this checklist so we can reproduce your issue:

  • Browser & version: (Example: Chrome 122, Safari 17)
  • Device & OS: (Example: MacBook Air macOS 14.3, iPadOS 17.4, Windows 11)
  • What you tried: (Start, Pause, Resume, changing speed, switching voice)
  • What happened: (No sound, voice list empty, playback stops at 30%)
  • Exact text length: (Rough word count or a short snippet helps)
  • Screenshots if relevant: (Especially for missing controls or errors)

Send the details to hello@read-aloud.com. For privacy, remove any sensitive text before sharing screenshots.

What to share (and what not to)

Helpful: device/OS versions, browser version, whether audio works on other sites, whether you tried another browser, and the size of your pasted text. These details point us to browser quirks faster.

Please do not send full legal documents, student records, or medical information. A short placeholder paragraph with the same length or characters (for example, “lorem ipsum” of equal size) is enough for us to test playback behavior.
