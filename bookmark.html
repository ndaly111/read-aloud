Bookmark Read‑Aloud

Save the tool so you can launch it with one tap. These steps keep the experience fast on laptops, tablets, and phones.

Desktop (Chrome, Edge, Firefox)

  • Open read-aloud.com/app.
  • Press Ctrl+D (Windows) or Cmd+D (macOS).
  • Choose the Bookmarks Bar and click Save. Drag it to the left for quicker access.

Pin the tab if you keep the tool open all day. Right-click the tab and choose Pin.

iPhone or iPad

  • Open read-aloud.com/app in Safari.
  • Tap the Share icon, scroll down, and tap Add to Home Screen.
  • Rename it to “Read‑Aloud” so you recognize it quickly.

This creates an app-like icon that launches directly into the tool with your last used settings.

Android

  • Open the tool in Chrome.
  • Tap the three dots menu and choose Add to Home screen.
  • Accept the prompt to install the shortcut.

If you use multiple profiles, create the shortcut in the profile that stores your preferred voice.

Want a bookmarklet?

Drag this link to your bookmarks bar: Open Read‑Aloud. When clicked, it opens the tool in the current tab. Bookmarklets cannot send page text into the tool for privacy reasons, but they make launching the player instant.

If you clear cookies or history often, re-pin the bookmark so it stays handy after cleanup.
