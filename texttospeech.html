Looking for the Text-to-Speech Reader?

The Read‑Aloud tool now lives at /app. We moved it to keep ads away from user-pasted text and to make room for a content-rich homepage.

This page remains as a waypoint for older bookmarks. You can still learn what changed and why the experience is now split between a content-rich landing page and a focused player.

Why the move?

Separating the tool from the landing page keeps the listening experience clean and focused. It also lets us explain privacy, voice setup, and troubleshooting on the homepage without cluttering the player.

What changed

  • Same controls and keyboard shortcuts, now in a dedicated layout.
  • New sidebar tips for privacy and copyright reminders.
  • Ads removed from the tool page; they remain on content pages like guides.

If you bookmarked the old retro design for nostalgia, you can still find the same functionality—just faster and better organized.

Start listening

Head to the tool, paste your text, and press Start. If you bookmarked this old URL, update your bookmark using the steps on the bookmark guide.

If you run into issues after switching, clear this old tab and open the tool fresh. All keyboard shortcuts and privacy defaults remain the same.

Your pasted text stays local; the move to /app did not change that privacy promise.
