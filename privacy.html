Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 July 2025

1. No text leaves your browser

All speech is produced with the Web Speech API or the open-source meSpeak engine. Your pasted or typed text is never sent to our servers.

2. Cookies & third-party ads

We display Google AdSense advertisements to pay for hosting. Google may store cookies or use local storage to serve personalized ads in accordance with their Advertising Policy.

You can opt-out of personalized ads by visiting adssettings.google.com.

3. Analytics

We use privacy-friendly Plausible Analytics (no cookies, no personal data) to see how many visitors we have and which pages are popular.

4. Contact information

If you have any privacy questions, email admin@read-aloud.com or use the contact form.

5. Changes

Policy updates will be posted on this page and take effect immediately.
