Audio Samples & Streams

Want to check your speakers before using the tool? Play the sample stream below and then switch to the in-browser player when you are ready.

Sample stream

This embedded stream helps you confirm your device volume before starting a Read‑Aloud session.

If the stream is blocked on a managed network, try connecting through a personal hotspot or skip straight to the player.

After the sound check

Once you know your speakers or headphones are working, open the Read‑Aloud tool and paste your text. The player uses system voices instead of this stream, so volume and tone may differ. Adjust your preferred voice and speed inside the player.

If you hear the stream but not the player, reload the page to trigger the browser’s audio permission prompt.

Why we keep audio local

The stream above is just a test. The text-to-speech player relies on voices installed on your device and does not send your text to a server. If you have trouble hearing the stream or the player, visit the Help Center for device-specific fixes.

Testing with a neutral stream also helps you rule out Bluetooth hiccups or system mute toggles before you paste important text. If the stream works but Read‑Aloud is silent, switch voices or browsers using the checklists in the Help Center to reload the device speech engine.
