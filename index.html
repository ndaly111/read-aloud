What is Read‑Aloud?

Read‑Aloud is a free text‑to‑speech tool that reads your text out loud directly in your browser. Paste anything you have the rights to use, pick a voice and speed, and press Start.

The tool is designed to be simple and distraction‑free. Many people use it for studying, proofreading, accessibility support, and language practice.
