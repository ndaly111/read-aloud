About Read-Aloud

Read-Aloud started in 2025 with one goal: make high-quality, distraction-free text-to-speech available to everyone, on any device, without forcing users to create an account or upload their documents.

What makes us different?

  • 100 % client-side processing – Your text never leaves your browser.
  • No paywalls – We fund hosting with lightweight, non-intrusive ads.
  • Accessibility first – WCAG-AA contrast, keyboard shortcuts and screen-reader-friendly labels.
  • Open tech stack – Web Speech API and open-source meSpeak for fallback.

Who runs the site?

Read-Aloud is maintained by Nick Daly, a knowledge-management analyst and accessibility enthusiast based in Maryland, USA. You can reach Nick directly via the contact form.
