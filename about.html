About This Tool

Our free text-to-speech (TTS) tool turns written words into clear, easy-to-listen audio. Whether you’re studying, multitasking, or navigating accessibility challenges, Read-Aloud offers a simple way to have text read aloud.

We built this tool to help students, professionals, and individuals with visual impairments or dyslexia. Using the latest in web speech APIs (or fallback audio from VoiceRSS), our site aims to provide accurate, convenient audio playback in multiple languages.

We also strive to keep this service lightweight, free, and privacy-friendly—no sign-ups required. For more tips on using TTS effectively, check out our blog.
