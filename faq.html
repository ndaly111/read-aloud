Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Last updated: December 2025

If your question is “something is broken,” start with Help & Troubleshooting. If your question is “how do I use this for school/work,” start with Guides.

Is Read‑Aloud free?

Yes. The tool is free to use and does not require an account.

Do you upload the text I paste?

No. Read‑Aloud uses your browser/OS speech engine; we do not send your pasted text to our servers.

What languages are supported?

Read‑Aloud supports any language/voice your device and browser provide. If you don’t see a voice for a language, install additional system voices in your device settings or try a different browser.

Why does my voice list look different in Chrome vs Safari?

Because voices are provided by the browser/OS. Different browsers may expose different voice lists. See Browser compatibility & voice availability.

I clicked “Start” but hear nothing on iPhone/iPad.

iOS is strict about audio playback. Check the Ring/Silent switch, disable Lockdown Mode, refresh, and tap Start again. See the full checklist on Help.

Can I download an MP3?

Not yet for system voices. Exporting MP3 from browser text‑to‑speech is harder than it looks. See Why MP3 export is hard.

How can I support the site?

  • Share the site with someone who would use it.
  • Buy me a coffee to help cover hosting.
